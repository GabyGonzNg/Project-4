# Project-4
The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) developed the National Risk Index (NRI) as a comprehensive tool to measure and compare natural hazard risk across different communities in the United States. Risk Averse, LLC, an independent risk analysis consulting research group, has asked our team to perform a sensitivity analysis that can review where biases may exist within the NRI scoring method. Additionally, they have asked us to create an alternative risk scoring method that can use the NRI dataset as an input. After creating this, we will compare the NRI to our risk index and determine if there are any categorical biases in how risk in scored for both methods.
